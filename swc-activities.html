Hi, Dear Friends,

Whether you’re feeling overwhelmed, need someone to talk to, or just want to work on your personal growth, know that the Student Wellness Centre (SWC) and Student Support Services (SSS) at IIT-B are here for you. These services are designed to help you thrive both academically and personally.

Student Wellness Centre (SWC):

Individual Counseling: If you ever need to talk through anything—whether it's personal, academic, or emotional— SWC's counselors are here to listen. It’s completely confidential.

Psychological Assessments: If you’re feeling stuck or overwhelmed, SWC offers professional assessments to help you better understand your mental health.

24/7 Free Online Counseling: Anytime you need support, you can access our confidential online counseling service, day or night.

External Counseling: If in-person counseling is more your style, you can attend sessions at recognized hospitals or centers, and the institute will cover the cost for you.

Student Support Services (SSS):

Personality Development Classes: Want to build confidence, leadership, or other life skills? These sessions are for you.

Language & Communication Skills: Need help with improving your writing or speaking skills? We've got you covered with workshops to enhance both verbal and written communication abilities to help you excel in both academic and professional environments.

Online Query and Grievance Portal: If you have any issues or concerns, you can easily reach out through the online portal - a dedicated platform to resolve any concerns or queries related to your student life.

Please don’t hesitate to use these resources—they’re here to support you. If you’d like more information or need help with anything, just get in touch with the Student Wellness Centre or visit the website.

Take care of yourselves!

With gratitude,
The Mental Health Survey Team
