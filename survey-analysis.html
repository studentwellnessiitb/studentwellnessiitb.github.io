Greetings, Wonderful Community,

Thank you for participating in our recent mental health survey! We wanted to share the key insights we have gathered from your responses, along with some graphs to give you a clear understanding of the findings.

Key Findings

Our survey revealed that:

  • 47% of students have moderate or severe anxiety.
  • 48% have moderate, moderately severe, or severe depression.
  • 80% have below average grit scores.
  • 75% possess a strong growth mindset, while 25% have a growth mindset with some fixed ideas.

We’re thrilled to report that no participants scored in the fixed mindset or fixed mindset with some growth ideas range! This reflects a strong inclination towards growth and adaptability among the IIT-B student community, which is truly commendable.

Take Care of Yourself

The survey results show that many of us are dealing with high levels of anxiety and depression. It’s a reminder that we’re all facing challenges, and taking care of your mental health is essential. Have you ever felt this way but weren’t sure whether to reach out for support? You're not alone in this feeling, and it’s completely okay to ask for help.

Whether your scores reflected anxiety, low grit, or any other concern, remember—this is a starting point, not a destination. There are always ways to improve, and reaching out for support is a sign of strength, not weakness.

As we work on ourselves, let’s also be mindful of the struggles others might be going through. A little kindness—whether to yourself or someone else—can make a big difference. We’re all in this together, and small acts of care can go a long way!
